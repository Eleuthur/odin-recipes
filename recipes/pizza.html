Description

An Italian classic that will be popular with everyone!.

Made with pleny of cheese and tasty Pepperone, this quick and easy pizza will appeal to all of your guests no matter their age.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Base
  • Pepperoni
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Mixed Herbs
  • More Cheese!!

Steps

  1. Cover the pizza base in a thin layer of the tomato sauce
  2. Sprinkle a quarter of the cheese over the base
  3. Spread the onions and Pepperoni around the base
  4. SPrinkle the herbs over the base
  5. Add the rest of the cheese
  6. Bake until the base is golden brown
