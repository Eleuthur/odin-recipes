Lasagne

Description

A tasty Italian classic dish certain to please all of your guests.

Made with pleny of cheese and hand-made pasta, this simple dish is easy to make and requires no advanced skills in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Beef Mince
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Creamy White Sauce
  • Basil
  • Mixed Herbs
  • More Cheese!!

Steps

  1. Brown Mince and onions
  2. Add Tomato Sauce
  3. Add Herbs
  4. Cook for 10 mins
  5. Layer Mince, Pasta Sheets & Creamy White Sauce
  6. Add Cheese
  7. Add more Cheese!
