Lasagne

Description

A warming soup that is sure to keep you cosy no matter the weather

This quick and simple soup makes a great starer, or a meal on it's own. Perfect with some crusty bread!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Onions
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Vegetable Stock

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the carrots
  2. Part cook the carrots and brocolli
  3. Bring the vegetable stock to a simmer
  4. Add the carrots and brocolli
  5. Add the onions and herbs
  6. Add the Tomatoes
  7. Cook for 45 mins
  8. Allow to cool slightly then blend with food processor
